Features of Word List

Offline Dictionary

We have added some of the most common words used in everyday life to the offline feature of our app, so that you can parse words even without an internet connection. We have also provided the power to favourite a word and add it to the quiz and take a self assessment quiz.

Add words to your list

If you are preparing for SAT or GRE, often you need to create your own list of words which are commonly asked and you can refer to them as and when you want to. Keeping that feature in mind we have come up with add words feature, which gives user the power to add their own words list.

New Words everyday

In this feature user is updated with new words every 24 hours along with its meaning and sentence. A push notification is send to all devices in which the app is installed and its useful for anybody who wants to expand their vocabulary.

Words on tile(WP)

This feature is available for windows phone only, it gives users the power to learn a new word without opening the app. Word is displayed on the live tile and when the tile flips the meaning and sentence is displayed. A new word is displayed every time user enters the app.

Random Words

This is an interesting feature that generates a new word from the web and displays it in the app. A random word from the web appears, if you know the meaning you can look for the next word, in case you don't then add it to your list and parse it later as you require.

Efficient search mechanism

When you search for a word its meaning and sentence is displayed along with origin and rhyming words. Using the concept of web mining this feature is made as powerful as possible, so as to provide you with best experience as possible. We have also integrated the app with cortana.

Search meaning of words from images

Image search is a powerful feature that gives the user the power to search for meaning of words from within the images. Using OCR user just have to click the image and extreact the text from the image, once text is extracted, user have to click on the word to know its meaning.

Pie chart anaysis

Pie chart analysis gives user the ability to analyse their self assessment quizzes, as a pie chart of quizzes taken from all the alphabets appear and user monitor which all alphabets needs more work and which all require less.
